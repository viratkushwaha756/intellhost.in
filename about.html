About IntelliHost

At IntelliHost, we specialize in providing a comfortable, secure, and well-managed living environment for students and paying guests. Since our inception in 2017, Intelli Host has been dedicated to offering high-quality accommodation solutions, making us a leading name in the hospitality industry. Our facilities are designed to meet the diverse needs of our residents, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free stay.

Our Services Include:

  • Accommodation: Spacious and well-furnished rooms, designed for comfort and convenience.
  • Security: Round-the-clock security, ensuring peace of mind for all residents.
  • Mess: Hygienic and nutritious meals, catered to the preferences of our residents.
  • Onboarding: Student induction program to help new students acclimate and integrate easily.
  • Community: Events, clubs, and workshops to foster engagement and personal development.
  • Student Experience:Comprehensive facility management,tech support,and a well-managed food mess.
  • Placement: Online assessments,training,mentoring,and placement assistance to ensure career success.
